ARABIC MEHANDI

Arabic Mehndi Concerns With The Impermanent Body Paint Especially On The Hands, Arms And Feet With The Mehndi Or So-Called Henna. Henna Is Actually A Tall Bush Or Small Size Tree, Which Is 2 To 6 Meter High

STYLISH MEHANDI

Stylish Mehandi Are Quite Simple Ones. Arabic Designs Mostly Consist Of Leaves, Veins, Flowers Etc. This Option Suits The Best If You Want To Extend The Design From Hand To Arm Or From Foot To Leg.

BRIDAL MEHANDI

For Any Indian Bride, Mehandi Is One Of The Most Auspicious Accessories. It Is One Of The '16 Shringhars' Of Indian Bridal Customs. It Does Have An Emotional Touch. It Has Most Special Day.
